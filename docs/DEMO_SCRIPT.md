## 2:05-2:25 - Shared, Not Isolated

Return to Crew Home and show saved family outputs. Switch from Lena to Jonas: response style changes, memory and artifacts remain shared.

## 2:25-2:42 - Product and Business Boundary

Show future Care, School, Admin and Party Corners plus clearly simulated premium and partner surfaces.

Open Judge View. Close with: one family memory, a Sidekick for every corner of family life, and real capabilities behind the characters.
